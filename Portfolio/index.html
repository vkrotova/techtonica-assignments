Violetta Krotova

After years of identifying talent and matching developers with companies, I’ve decided to flip the script and become the developer myself. It’s been a thrilling and challenging transition, but the path from tech recruiter to coder is one I’m excited to be walking. I’ll be sharing my journey—what I’ve learned, the resources I’ve found most helpful, and how my time in recruiting has shaped the way I approach coding. Whether you're thinking of making a similar leap, already a developer, or just curious about the behind-the-scenes of tech recruitment, I hope this space serves as a useful and inspiring resource. Let’s build something great together!
